Meetr

DeltaHacks 2016

Meetr is an application aimed to help businesses conserve more energy. It allows users to book meeting rooms using the Android Application. Once a meeting room is booked, the Node.js service will automatically make requests to a Nest smart thermostat, prior to the meeting, to warm up the booked room and then will automatically disable the heating once the meeting is completed. Winner of the GE Sustainability Prize.
Check it out on GitHub
