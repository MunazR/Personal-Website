About Me

My name is Munaz Rahman and I am a student with an amazing passion for technology pursuing a Bachelor of Applied Science in Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo.

My passion for technology began when I picked up a book titled Look Mom! I Built My Own Web Site from my local library. I immediately fell in love with the ability to design and create something of my own by writing lines of code. This truly sparked my love for programming so I continued to advance my skills in other programming languages.

On top of programming I love building things using technology. I've participated in various robotic clubs, love attending hackathons and also working on projects of my own. I truly believe that technology, both through software and hardware, can be used to solve many problems faced by people today.

I am currently in a co-operative education program and am constantly looking for new work opportunties. Take a look at my CV for more information on my past experience and what I can bring to your company. Please feel free to contact me with any questions.
